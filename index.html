Prompt:

Write a summary for senior marketing leaders of these findings on the persuasive nature of VR and AR video advertisements.

Summary generated by AI:

A conceptual review suggests that virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) video advertisements can persuade consumer attitudes. Incorporating VR and AR technology can help build stronger brands, engage audiences, and provide unique experiences for customers using mobile devices.

Six research propositions were identified to examine consumer attitude persuasion based on the literature. Variables like advertisement quality, demographic differences, and technological context in VR and AR ads facilitate central route persuasion. Meanwhile, factors like source credibility, social presence, and message content influence persuasion through the peripheral route. The study presents a method to analyze consumer attitudes using the ELM theory.

*This summary came from an article titled "The persuasion effects of virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) video advertisements: A conceptual review."

Prompt:

Summarize the impact of AI-generated content on modern journalism.

Summary generated by AI:

AI-generated content is transforming journalism by automating news writing, enhancing data-driven insights, and enabling real-time reporting. This shift reduces manual workload, allowing journalists to focus on investigative reporting and creative storytelling.

However, challenges like misinformation, bias in AI models, and ethical concerns about authorship persist. Effective AI-human collaboration in newsrooms is essential to balance efficiency with editorial integrity.

*This summary is based on a report from "The Future of AI in Journalism: Trends & Challenges."
